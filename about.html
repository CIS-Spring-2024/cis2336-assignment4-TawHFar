About Sugarland Cougar Cafe

Welcome to Sugarland Cougar Cafe, a cafeteria more than just a place to eat but a hub where students, faculty and staff come together to eat delicious food and create long lasting memories.With a talented group of chefs crafting an array of mouthwatering dishes from all over the world and a commitment to freshness, quality, and diversity. We aim to cultivate a welcoming enviroment and providing nourishment all around for the UH student body. We look forward to seeing you!

Meet the Owner

Tawhida is proud visionary behind Sugarland Cougar Cafe, with a passion and profound love for culinary excellence she has made it her plan to share her love with other students. Born and raised in Houston and background with Egyptian cuisine she developed a fascination with the art of cooking for a rich and loving community. Driven by the commitment to sourcing the freshest,highest quality ingredients, Tawhida continues to work to ensure the aspect of the dining expiernce reflects the dedication. Driven by a relentless pursuit of perfection and a genuine desire to delight and inspire their guests, Tawhida invites you to join them on a culinary journey unlike any other.For every student and staff wanting to expierence a memorable dining experience, Tawhida and the entire team look forward to welcoming you with open arms and inviting you to savor the flavors of Sugarland Cougar Cafe in a whole new light.
